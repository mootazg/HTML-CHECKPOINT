Cast

Main characters:

Walter White
The anti-hero of Breaking Bad who went from a basic chemistry teacher to a drug empire owner.
Jesse Pinkman
He is a crystal meth cook and dealer who works with his former high school chemistry teacher, Walter White.
Saul Goodman
Saul Goodman is a lawyer based in Albuquerque, New Mexico.
Gustavo Fring aka Gus
Gus is a Chilean-American restaurant entrepreneur and major narcotics distributor who primarily worked in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Originally collaborating with the Mexican drug cartel to distribute cartel cocaine.
